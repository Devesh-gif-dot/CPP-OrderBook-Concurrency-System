=========================================
     FILE 1: CORE DATA MODELS PLAN
=========================================

Class: Account
--------------
Purpose: Tracks a user's funds in memory.
Attributes:
- account_id (Integer)
- balance (Double): Positive means cash, negative means debt.
- active_order_ids (Hash Set): Quick lookup of orders this user currently has open.

Struct: Order
-------------
Purpose: Represents a single buy or sell request.
Attributes:
- order_id (Integer)
- account_id (Integer)
- type (Enum: BID or ASK)
- price (Double)
- quantity (Integer)
- timestamp (Long Integer): Microsecond resolution for exact time placement.

Struct: LtpTracker
------------------
Purpose: Tracks the Minimum and Maximum Last Traded Price (LTP) for the current 1-second window [i, i+1).
Attributes:
- current_second (Long Integer): The Unix epoch time in seconds.
- min_price (Double): Lowest trade price in this second.
- max_price (Double): Highest trade price in this second.
